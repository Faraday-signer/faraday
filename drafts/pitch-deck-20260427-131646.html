The Solution

Faraday is a signer.
Not a wallet. A signer.

Rule 01
Always offline
No USB. No WiFi. No Bluetooth. Transactions move in and out only as QR codes. A fully infected computer cannot reach it.
Rule 02
Stateless
No keys when off. You bring your seed. The device signs. You turn it off. The device forgets. Nothing to steal — even if someone takes it.
Rule 03
Open source
All firmware, all hardware. Anyone audits. Anyone builds. You don't trust us — you check.
