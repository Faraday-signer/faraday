The Solution

We don't sell hardware.
We sell the whole system.

A small device that never connects to the internet, a browser app that talks to every Solana platform, a dashboard for company accounts, and an indexer that finds them in milliseconds. Together they show you, in plain English, exactly what you are about to approve — before you approve it.
The signer
Small device, always offline. Reads QR codes. Shows the real transaction in plain English.
Browser app
Works with Phantom, Squads, Jupiter, Drift — every Solana platform. Plug-and-play.
Team dashboard
Company accounts, payroll, multiple approvers. Each one signs on their own Faraday.
Account finder
Sub-100 ms lookup of every shared account you belong to. Closes a gap nothing else fills.
●Always offline — never connects, anywhere
●Forgets when off — no keys to steal
●Open source — anyone reads the code
●Built in Rust — no buffer overflows, no crashes
